Nutrición y Salud

SELF INITIATED UX CASE STUDY 2022

Challenge:

Some users want to lose weight, but some of them are busy most of the day or don't know how to start because they think this might be difficult, but they need more information about it. The city where they live is small, so they don't have many options. But with the website, people can get more information and start with a date and have a healthy life.

The goal:

Design a website where users can get a good option if they want to start losing weight in a proper and healthy way. In the city, this is a good tool because they don’t have many options to find a nutritionist. This website can help them and be guided by a professional.

My role:

As a UX designer, I am designing a website for mobile, tablet, and desktop from beginning to end.
