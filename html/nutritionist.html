Villa Mia's Party Salon Website

Challenge:

Villa Mia's Party Salon is a local place where people can celebrate their parties. They offer a good environment and food for users. Villa Mia's Party Salon target people who want to find a good place via the internet, because in the city some of the locations don't have any, and this is important for users, they want to know about it before asking.

The Goal:

Design a website for Villa Mia's Party Salon that allows users to easily organize and know the place, and even they can see the meals that it offers.

My role:

As a UX designer, I am leading the Villa Mia's Party Salon website design.
